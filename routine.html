Evening Routine

Here’s an effective evening routine to help you sleep better at night:

1. Wind Down (7:00 - 8:00 PM)

  • Have a light dinner (avoid heavy, greasy, or spicy foods).
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol intake.
  • Do some light stretching or a short walk to relax your body.

2. Reduce Screen Time (8:00 - 9:00 PM)

  • Dim the lights in your home.
  • Reduce phone, tablet, or TV use (blue light disrupts melatonin).
  • Read a physical book or listen to calming music instead.

3. Relaxation Time (9:00 - 9:30 PM)

  • Take a warm shower or bath to lower body temperature.
  • Try deep breathing, meditation, or gentle yoga.
  • Write in a journal or do gratitude reflection.

4. Sleep Preparation (9:30 - 10:00 PM)

  • Set your room to a cool (65-70°F) and dark environment.
  • Use blackout curtains and keep noise levels low.
  • Use calming scents like lavender or chamomile.

5. Bedtime (10:00 PM)

  • Get in bed at the same time every night.
  • Avoid checking your phone—use a traditional alarm clock instead.
  • Try progressive muscle relaxation if you struggle to fall asleep.
