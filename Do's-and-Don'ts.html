Your sleep quality depends on what you do and don’t do before sleeping. To get better sleep, follow this dos and don’ts list.

✅ Top 5 Dos Before Bed:

  • Stick to a Sleep Schedule – Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Limit Screen Time – Avoid blue light from phones, tablets, and TVs at least 30–60 minutes before bed.
  • Create a Relaxing Routine – Read, meditate, or do deep breathing to wind down.
  • Keep Your Room Cool and Dark – Ideal temperature: 60–67°F (15–19°C). Use blackout curtains if needed.
  • Avoid Caffeine and Heavy Meals – Stop consuming caffeine at least 6 hours before bed and avoid eating large meals late at night.

❌ Top 5 Don'ts Before Bed:

  • Avoid Using Your Phone or Watching TV – Blue light and stimulating content disrupt sleep.
  • Don’t Drink Too Much Water – Prevent frequent bathroom trips that interrupt sleep.
  • Avoid Intense Exercise Right Before Bed – It can increase alertness and body temperature.
  • Don’t Stress or Have Heavy Conversations – Anxiety and overthinking can make it hard to sleep.
  • Avoid Alcohol and Nicotine – Both disrupt sleep quality, even if they make you feel drowsy initially.
